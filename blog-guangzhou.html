JinTuDi LingFeng Company Participates in the 136th Canton Fair!

  • Dean Yan
  • Nov 4, 2024

JinTuDi LingFeng Company proudly participated in the 136th Canton Fair, showcasing our premium tomato sauce products at our independent exhibition booth. During the event, we engaged in fruitful business discussions with over 80 potential partners, highlighting the quality and versatility of our products.

We also had the pleasure of warmly hosting our long-time partners, many of whom introduced us to their friends and colleagues, helping us expand our network and explore new business opportunities.

Reuniting with old friends and meeting new ones are both things that bring joy.

Leo's father in law, the owner of the factory also came.

Looks familiar right?

We have the youngest new friend, first time to China.

Also signed some orders, just like bonus!

The fair provided an excellent platform for strengthening existing relationships while building new ones, further solidifying our position in the global market. See you 2025 on the 137th/138th Canton Fair Guangzhou!
